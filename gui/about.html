About VidCaptio

What is VidCaptio?

VidCaptio is a free and open-source video captioning software that enables you to add multi-language captions to videos. It utilizes OpenAI's Whisper for speech recognition and ffmpeg for video processing.

  • Multi-Language Captioning: VidCaptio allows users to add captions in multiple languages to their videos.
  • Flexible Captioning Options: Users have the option to choose which languages they want to add captions for.
Works best for videos with English audio. For other languages, you might need a device with a GPU or stronger specs.

How to Use VidCaptio

Using VidCaptio is simple and straightforward. Here's a step-by-step guide:

  1. Open VidCaptio and create a new project (File > New project).
  2. Choose the language of the audio in the video.
  3. Click generate captions.
  4. Make any edits you would like in editor.
  5. Click Apply captions.
  6. You can check captioned video by navigating to Open > Captioned video
  7. Save project.
Note: You might not be able to enable captions in some video players. However, you can always use the caption files saved in the project folder.
